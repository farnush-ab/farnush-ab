— The Concept

A six-thousand metre sanctuary for considered design.

Located within the prestigious Palladium Mall, Design Station is a 6,000 m² sanctuary dedicated to the fusion of luxury living, smart home technology, and architectural precision.

As a distinguished initiative by Farmand, it represents an elevated approach to intelligent design — where every fixture is chosen not for its catalogue value but for the room it will eventually inhabit.

The Social Table — a dedicated space for dialogue and collaboration, where architects bring clients and conversations become commissions.
